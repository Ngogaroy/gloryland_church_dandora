About Gloryland Church

Our Story, Our Faith, Our Community in Dandora.

Our Journey in Dandora

Gloryland Church, Dandora Assembly, began with a humble vision in **1995**, born from a deep desire to establish a spiritual home that would serve the unique needs of the Dandora community in Nairobi. Founded by **Pastor John Mumo**, the early days were marked by fervent prayer meetings and small gatherings in local homes.

Over the years, as God faithfully added to our numbers, we transitioned from rented spaces to our current dedicated location in Dandora Area IV. Each step of our journey has been a testament to God's grace and the unwavering commitment of our members. We have grown not just in size, but in our capacity to impact lives through discipleship, community outreach, and unwavering worship. Our story is still being written, and we believe the best is yet to come for Gloryland Church and for Dandora.

We continue to uphold the foundational principles of faith, love, and service that characterized our beginnings, adapting our methods to remain relevant and impactful in a dynamic urban environment.

What We Believe

Our core tenets that guide our faith and practice.

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, our supreme and final authority in all matters of faith and conduct.

One God, Three Persons

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son (Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit, co-equal in power and glory.

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ's atoning sacrifice on the cross, not by works.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, enabling believers to live a godly life and empowering them for service.

The Church

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in Christ, forming the universal Church, His body, and in the importance of local church fellowship.

Second Coming

We believe in the blessed hope of Jesus Christ's personal and imminent return to Earth in power and glory.

Our Guiding Principles

Defining who we are and where we are heading as a church family.

Our Mission

To equip believers for works of service, foster deep spiritual growth, and proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ with love and power to the people of Dandora, Nairobi, and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Dandora, a thriving spiritual family where lives are changed, communities are uplifted, and God's glory is revealed through every member.
